There are many reasons to care about why an animal isn't abused, neglected or forced into extinction. Our own survival depends on the ecosystem around us and the animals who inhabit them. Turtles, as with all creatures, are a gift to humans and should be treated as such. Many have drawn attention to the virtues of better care and how we can all help preserve the turtles around us.
