I have snorkeled around the Caribbean and seen some beautiful things in those clear waters. Did you know: Turtle spotting isn't a feature of every snorkel or dive destination. Bonaire is a stunning island for ecotourism and turtle viewing because it is considered by some the best diving and snorkeling in the world. Visit 1,000 steps for an unforgettable experience.

It's sister island of Curacao is known for hidden gems also, including a beach for guaranteed turtle encounters. I've compiled what I believe to be the highlights of where you can find our favorite friends. Whether you travel near or far, there should be a destination in this list that will move you to book your next vacation.
